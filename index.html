Welcome to the Xeon Club Website!

This is the official website used to give information and link media associated to the servers.

First of all, What is Xeon Club?

Xeon Club is an Intel-themed GDPS, being a rebirth of the old Lucky GDPS with it's peaking 110 members, with it's Discord server being made on May 08, 2021, owned by Luk3#3264


And what is a GDPS?

GDPS, or Geometry Dash Private Server, is a Geometry Dash server which is completely separated from the Boomlings one (The one GD is hosted).
It means that levels, accounts and pretty much everything is different from the Boomlings server.
